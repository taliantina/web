Japan

Japan, or the State of Japan, is a country made up of 6852 islands located in eastern Asia, in the Pacific Ocean. The name Japan means "the origin of the sun". For this reason this country is also known as the land of the rising sun.

Japan's boundaries are: to the west the Sea of Japan, China, North Korea, South Korea and Russia. To the north it borders the Sea of Okhotsk, to the south the East China Sea and Taiwan, and to the east it borders the Pacific Ocean.

The capital of Japan is Tokyo and is located on the island of Honshū. It is also home to the largest number of inhabitants in relation to surface area in the world; more than 30 million people live in Tokyo.

Japan currently has a total population of more than 127 million inhabitants, although this number is decreasing year after year, which means that it maintains strict birth control to avoid exceeding the historical population index of this country.
